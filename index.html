Our Mission

Defend the weak and the fatherless; uphold the cause of the poor and oppressed, to rescue the weak and the needy; deliver them from the hand of the wicked.

Psalms 82:3-4

Over the years, this has manifested itself in many different programs and locales including rescuing victims of sex trafficking, running orphanages and schools, drilling water wells, managing farms, micro-lending for economic development, establishing clinics and hospitals, and much more. We have demonstrated a penchant for spearheading attacks on the worst societal issues in the most hopeless situations. However, we need your help to continue to support and grow these endeavors.
